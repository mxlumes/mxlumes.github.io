The Protocol

Step 01: Weigh

Place cup on scale. Pour 3 parts Resin, then 1 part Hardener.

Step 02: Mix

Mix slowly for 3 minutes. Scrape sides and bottom thoroughly.

Step 03: Pour

Pour into mold. Wait 5 mins for bubbles to rise, then torch.
