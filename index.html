The Pour Protocol

Follow our molecular binding process for perfect results.

01

Measure & Mix

Weigh accurately by weight (3:1). Mix slowly for exactly 3 minutes, scraping sides and bottom.

02

Rest & Pour

Let resin sit for 2 minutes to allow micro-bubbles to rise. Pour slowly into the lowest point of the mold.

03

Torch & Cover

Pass a torch quickly over the surface to pop tension bubbles. Cover to prevent dust settling.

Safety & Best Practices

✓ The Dos
  • Wear Nitrile gloves and a respirator mask.
  • Work in a well-ventilated room (22°C - 25°C).
  • Mix scrape the sides of your cup thoroughly.
  • Seal porous surfaces (wood) before pouring.
✕ The Don'ts
  • Do not mix by volume! Use a digital scale.
  • Do not scrape the cup when pouring into the mold.
  • Avoid adding more than 6% liquid pigment.
  • Do not let water or moisture touch uncured resin.
